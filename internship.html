Internship Search

Find an Internship That Launches Your Career

Internships are one of the most valuable ways to gain real-world experience, explore career paths, and build professional connections. Whether you’re searching for your first internship or preparing for competitive applications, these resources will help guide you through every step of the process.

On this page, you’ll find practical guidance, tools, and opportunities tailored to School of Information students and aligned with industry hiring practices.

Need personalized advice? You can always meet with a CDO Career Coach to refine your search strategy and application materials.

Finding Internship opportunities

To Find internship opportunities, consider the following strategies:

  • Utilize the UMSI Career Development Office resources, including job boards and internship listings.
  • Network with alumni and industry professionals through LinkedIn and UMSI events.
  • Attend career fairs and workshops to connect with potential employers.
  • Leverage your coursework and projects to identify relevant internship opportunities.

Also consider visiting UMSI Careerlink, which offers on-demand access to career support: internships, jobs, career coaching, career-building resources, special events and more.

  • UMSI Careerlink

Prepare Your Application Materials

Strong application materials are essential to securing an internship. A polished resume highlights your skills and experiences, while a well-structured portfolio showcases your projects and design process. Keeping your LinkedIn profile updated and professional also helps recruiters find and connect with you. Together, these materials create a clear picture of who you are and what you can contribute.

Career Fairs

Career fairs are valuable opportunities for students to meet employers, recruiters, and industry professionals in one place. These events allow you to learn about organizations, discover internship openings, and make meaningful connections that can lead to future interviews and job offers. Whether you are just beginning your search or actively applying, career fairs provide direct access to companies looking for students like you.

How Career Fairs Support Your Internship Search

  • Connect Directly with Employers
    Career fairs give you face-to-face time with recruiters who are actively seeking interns. These conversations help you stand out beyond online applications.
  • Discover Internship Opportunities
    Many companies use career fairs to promote open internship roles. Attending helps you learn about positions that may not be widely advertised.
  • Build Professional Confidence
    Speaking with recruiters helps you practice introducing yourself, discussing your experience, and articulating your career interests.
  • Grow Your Network
    Even brief conversations can lead to valuable professional contacts. Maintaining these connections can open doors to future opportunities.
