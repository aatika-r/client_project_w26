Networking & Connections

Build Your Professional Network

Networking is a powerful tool for career development, providing opportunities to connect with industry professionals, alumni, and peers. Building a strong network can open doors to internships, job opportunities, mentorship, and valuable insights into your chosen field. Whether you are new to networking or looking to expand your existing connections, these resources will help you navigate the process with confidence.

On this page, you'll find strategies for effective networking, tips for leveraging LinkedIn, and guidance on attending events and reaching out to professionals. Remember, networking is about building genuine relationships—focus on mutual value and long-term connections.

Need personalized advice? You can always meet with a CDO Career Coach to refine your networking strategy and practice your outreach skills.

Why Networking Matters

  • Learn about job and internship opportunities before they are publicly posted
  • Gain insight into different career paths and industries
  • Receive advice on resumes, portfolios, and interviews
  • Build relationships that may lead to referrals
  • Develop confidence in professional communication

Ways to Connect

  • Attend career fairs and employer information sessions
  • Join UMSI student organizations and interest groups
  • Connect with alumni through LinkedIn and UMSI networks
  • Participate in workshops and networking events hosted by the CDO
  • Schedule informational interviews with professionals

Online Networking Tools

Maintaining a professional online presence helps recruiters and alumni find and connect with you. These platforms can support your networking efforts:

  • LinkedIn – Build your professional profile and connect with alumni
  • UMSI CareerLink – Access career events, employer sessions, and alumni connections

Tips for Effective Networking

  1. Prepare a short introduction about yourself
  2. Ask thoughtful questions and listen actively
  3. Follow up with new contacts after meeting
  4. Keep your LinkedIn profile updated
  5. Stay in touch with your network over time

Need Guidance?

Not sure where to start? The UMSI Career Development Office offers networking workshops, alumni panels, and one-on-one coaching to help you build strong professional connections.
