Portfolios

Craft a Portfolio that Showcases Your Work

Your portfolio is more than a collection of projects — it’s a narrative of how you think, solve problems, and design for people. Whether you’re building your first UX portfolio or refining an existing one, these resources will help you create a compelling, professional showcase of your work. UMSI students are known for thoughtful, human-centered design and technical rigor. A clear, well-structured portfolio helps you stand out to recruiters, hiring managers, and graduate program reviewers. On this page, you’ll find practical guidance, templates, and examples tailored to School of Information students and aligned with industry expectations. Need feedback? You can always meet with a CDO Career Coach to review your portfolio and presentation strategy.
